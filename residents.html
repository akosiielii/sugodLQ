Historical population


The population of Sugod grew from 792 in 1990 to 1,336 in 2020, an increase of 544 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 1.95%, or a decrease of 131 people, from the previous population of 1,467 in 2015.
